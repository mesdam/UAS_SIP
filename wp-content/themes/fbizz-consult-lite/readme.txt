FBizz Consult Lite WordPress has used for most of all type of business including Business consultant, news, portfolio, blogs, etc. Its used for the multipurpose business like Marketing, Investment business, IT infrastructure, Consultant, Manufacture plant, Retailer, Wholesaler, Online business, Insurance, SIP, Mutual Fund, Services, Marketing, Finance, Stock Market, Nifty, Store business, IT Firm, Retailers, Education, Shopping, E-commerce, Gift Service market, Beauty, Fashion, Blogger, News, Portfolio and many more business. This Lite WordPress theme is cross-browser compatible, fully responsive, translation ready & SEO friendly.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to customize theme FBizz Consult Lite? =

Just click on Appearance -> Customize
Now you can customize all section in theme

= How to Upgrade theme to pro ?=
Just click on Appearance -> Customize 
Now find upgrade to pro button, just click on it to upgrade pro version

= How to work on Header Phone and Address =
Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> Header Phone and Address

= How to work on Header social links =
Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> Social Link

= How to work on Banner Text? =
Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> Home Banner Text

= How to work on Resource section? =
Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> Resource section

= How to work on Footer copyright ? =
Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> Footer
